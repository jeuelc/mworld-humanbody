Why do we sleep?

Ever tried to stay awake long past your bedtime and suddenly woken up without realising you had even fallen asleep? As much as you might try, you cannot fight sleep. If your body needs sleep, you will fall asleep.

Sleep is a biological process, meaning that our bodies control it without us realising it, much like the way our bodies control our digestion system. Our bodies also protect sleep by forcing you to sleep, even if you don't want to.

Sleep is controlled by two connected systems. The first is called the circadian rhythm and the second is called the homeostatic drive.
