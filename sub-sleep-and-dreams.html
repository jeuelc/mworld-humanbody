Sleep and Dreams

Dogs do it. Dolphins do it. Ants do it. We do it. Even dinosaurs did it. Every living thing on Earth sleeps in one way or another.

But why do we sleep? And why do we dream? These are still great mysteries of science.

We do know that our bodies need sleep just like we need food and water. Without sleep, we cannot survive. Sleep is important for learning, playing sport, staying healthy and being happy.

So how do we know if we are getting the sleep our body and brain needs? What happens if we don't get enough sleep? And why is it that some people can remember their dreams but others can't?
