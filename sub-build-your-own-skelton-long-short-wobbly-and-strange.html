Bones come in all different shapes and sizes, and are designed to fit a particular need.

Long bones form the limbs and are essential for mobility. One example is the all-powerful leg bone, the femur! Short bones are more cube-shaped, and you need them for balance and stability. They form the base of your hands and feet; you couldn't do push ups or play sport without them.

Flat bones are just that, flat. They typically protect your internal organs like your lungs, heart and brain. Sesamoid bones are usually short or irregular bones, embedded in a tendon. Can you think of any bones you can wobble? They will be your sesamoid bones!

And then there are the irregular bones that don't fit into any category. They are really unique and some of them shape your face! Can you think of any others?
