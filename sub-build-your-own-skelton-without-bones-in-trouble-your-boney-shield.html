Your Bony Shield

One of the most important organs in your body is your brain. It is the control center of your body and that is why you have a skull around it, to protect it.

Your spinal cord connects your brain to the rest of your body. The bones that are called your vertebrae protect your spinal cord.

Other vital organs are located in your trunk. Your heart and lungs are really important for you to breathe. Your stomach, intestines and kidneys are really important for you to digest the food you eat. These organs are protected by the bones of the rib cage.

All these very important functions of our bodies would be at risk without bony protection. What do you think would happen if you didn't have bones to protect your brain, spinal cord, heart and other organs?
