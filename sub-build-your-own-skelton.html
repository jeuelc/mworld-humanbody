Build your own skeleton

Every person has an internal skeleton. Even fish, reptiles and birds have skeletons!

Your skeleton is made of a collection of bones that holds your body up. These bones help you move, protect your internal organs, and even produce red and white blood cells!

Your bones are all very much alive, unlike like the ones you see in museums, which are dead! Like other parts of your body, your bones are growing and changing all the time.

Find out about the different types of bones you have and what they are made of, how to give yourself the best possible skeleton and even build your own skeleton!
