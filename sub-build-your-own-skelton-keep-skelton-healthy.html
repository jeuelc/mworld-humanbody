Keep your skeleton healthy

Because your bones are alive, and are growing and repairing, they have special requirements to help them do this!

There are two things they need: proper exercise to help them grow stronger and the right foods and drinks to give them the fuel to do their job!

Your peak bone mass (the point in life at which your bones are most dense) is achieved in your early twenties. However, the strength of your bones when you're an adult is determined by whether you do the RIGHT EXERCISE and eat the RIGHT FOODS in your TEENAGE YEARS.

If you don't do this correctly when you are a teenager, you will have weak bones as an adult, and then it will be too late to do anything about it.

So if you are a teenager now or soon will be, you have a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to form YOUR BODY'S BEST SKELETON!
