Without bones, we're in trouble

What do you think your body would be like if you didn't have any bones? You would be a lump of organs and skin. You would have no support to stand up, walk or lift a drink to your mouth.

Bones are very important to protect your vital organs like your lungs, heart and brain! Your bones also work with your muscles to help you move around.

Your bones also make blood. The innermost part of many of your bones is called the bone marrow cavity and this is where blood cells are made.
