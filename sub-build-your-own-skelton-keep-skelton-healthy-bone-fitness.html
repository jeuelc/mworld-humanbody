Bone fitness

To keep your bones fit as you grow older, you need to exercise. This puts weight on your bones and is called bone loading. Putting these forces on your bones makes them grow and remodel during your teenage years, helping them become stronger when you are older.

If you don't do enough weight bearing exercise when you are a teenager, your adult bones will be much weaker and more likely to break and fracture then someone who did do these things.
