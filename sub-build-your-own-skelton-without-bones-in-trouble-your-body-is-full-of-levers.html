Your body is full of levers

 
click here click here

You have many levers in your body. These levers are in the form of bones and muscles and they help your body move.

When you nod your head, your bones and muscles are working together to move your skull. This is very similar to a see-saw in a playground.

When you stand on your tip-toes, your bones and muscles in your feet help you raise your body. This is very similar to a wheelbarrow.

When you drink a glass of water, the muscles in your arm work to pull your hand bones to grab the glass and drink the water. The elbow and shoulder joints allow your arm to move freely.
