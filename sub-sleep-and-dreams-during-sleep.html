What happens during sleep?

 

Even though when you sleep, you lay still with your eyes closed, so many exciting and important things happen inside your brain and body! The way you feel when you're awake depends on what happens while you're sleeping.

Your body is working hard to help you grow taller, repair your tissues and muscles and even help you fight illness! Your brain is putting everything you have learned during the day into memory and is also preparing your brain to work properly for the next day! It helps you pay attention and improves your learning; from learning a new dance routine, how to kick a football or even how to play the piano!
